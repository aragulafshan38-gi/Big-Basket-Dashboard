# Big-Basket-Dashboard
1. Big Basket Sales Dashboard
  
2. Short desceiption and purpose:
This BigBasket Sales Dashboard built in Power BI provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, outlet operations, and customer satisfaction.
It combines insights from multiple dimensions — such as outlet type, size, location, and item categories — to help management make data-driven business decisions.

3. Tech Stack
   .  Excel (For data transformation, cleaning, merging, and pre-processing)
   .  Power BI (For Dashboard, Visualization, and DAX calculations)

4. Features and Highlights:
   .  KPI Overview Cards
   .  Interactive Data Visualizations
   .  Outlet Type & Size Analysis
   .  Category-wise Product Insights
   .  Data Integration & Modeling
   .  User-Friendly Design
   .  Built with Power BI & Excel

5. Key Questions:
   . What is the total sales revenue generated across all outlets?
   . Which product categories (e.g., Fruits & Vegetables, Snacks, Household) contribute most to total sales?
   . How have sales evolved from 2010 to 2022?
   . What’s selling best?
   . Where are sales strongest?
   . Why are some outlets outperforming others?

6. Business Impact and insights:
   .  Increase sales
   .  Reduce operational costs
   .  Improve customer satisfaction
   .  Make smarter investments
   .  Drive growth in high-potential markets
